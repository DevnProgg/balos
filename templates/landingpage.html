About Us

Dona@balos chef School and hospitality is a private owned culinary School situated in Maseru Lesotho. It was founded by Chef Donald Moletsane in 2013.It is currently the only school of its kind in the mountain kingdom. Dona@balos is legally recognized with the government of Lesotho. It is a trusted skills development organization. Now Dona@balos is providing a program for students who wish to become vibrant professionals

See What Our School Offers & What We Provide

Diploma

This is a program that is built to shape both the student’s creativity and ability to handle kitchen pressure, and hard work. The diploma program is a two year program in which students spend 18 months of intensive training, and then move on to 6 months of internship and hard field work.


You'll Learn

Method of cooking | Hygiene | Menu planning and budgeting | Food cost | Culinary fundamentals | Food handling | Serving, platting and garnishing| Baking | Knife skills| Nutrition| Food and Beverages .


Requirments

CERTIFICATE- For entry into Dona@Balos chef school, candidates have to posses a junior certificate (JC) pass higher. DIPLOMA— For entry into the diploma program, candidates have to posses a C.O.S.E pass or higher. OR posses a JC pass with Skills Development program. The skills development program requires that a candidate posses a pass in Junior Certificate.
